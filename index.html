--- layout: page title: Brett & Jacyln Reierson ---

Brett and Jacyln Reierson are bringing life to the people of DR Congo in Africa by sharing God's love through avaiation and technology. As a pilot and flight mechanic, Brett flies goods and personnel to service the needs of communities in rural locations through Mission Aviation Fellowship (MAF). Jacyln nurtures and teaches her young children as well as uses her skills as a Registered Nurse and Instructor to help people in need.
